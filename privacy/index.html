Privacy Policy — OuiSi App

Effective July 16, 2026

This policy covers the OuiSi iPhone app, made by OuiSi Inc. (Hudson Valley, New York). The short version: we collect only what the game needs to work, we store it with Apple, we don't sell it, and we don't use ads or trackers.

What we collect

  • Sign-in identity. You sign in with your Apple ID. We receive an anonymous identifier from Apple, plus your name if you choose to share it. You choose the display name and initials other players see.
  • Photos you add to games. Photos are the game. When you add one, it's uploaded so the other players in that game can see it.
  • Comments you write. The conversations attached to photo connections in your games.
  • Game records. Which games exist, who's playing in them, and when things happened.
  • A push notification token, so your phone can be told when someone adds a photo or comment to your game.

Where it lives

Everything above is stored in Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) service. We don't operate our own servers, and your photos and comments never pass through anything other than your device and Apple's infrastructure. Your device also keeps a local copy of recent photos and comments so games open quickly.

How it's used

Solely to run the game: showing your photos and comments to the other players in your games, and sending game notifications. That's the whole list.

What we don't do

  • No selling or sharing your data with anyone outside your games
  • No advertising and no ad networks
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No reading your photo library — the app only receives photos you explicitly add

Who sees your content

The photos and comments you add to a game are visible to the people in that game. Invite links contain a game code — anyone who has the app and the link can join that game, so share links only with people you want playing.

Deleting things

Deleting a game from the app permanently removes its photos and comments for every player. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us (below) and we'll take care of it.

Age

OuiSi is for adults. The app is rated 17+ and is not directed at children; we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 17.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the date above. Questions or deletion requests: reach us through the contact page at ouisi.co.
